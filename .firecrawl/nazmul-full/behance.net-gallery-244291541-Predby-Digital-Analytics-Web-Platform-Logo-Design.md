Client Project

Predby - Secure & Trust-Driven Digital Prediction Platform Brand Identity

Predby is a modern digital platform designed for making future-oriented value predictions in a simple, secure, and trustworthy environment. The logo is built around an upright, forward-moving arrow, symbolizing: Growth & progress, Accuracy & direction, Confidence in decision-making, Positive forward momentum

The arrow form subtly represents insight and upward movement, reinforcing the platform’s purpose of helping users visualize outcomes with clarity and confidence. A clean abstract structure was chosen instead of a letter-based mark to ensure flexibility, memorability, and long-term scalability across digital interfaces.
