About

AutoNex– Vehicle Service & Repair Brand Identity

AutoNex is a professional vehicle service and repair brand focused on precision, reliability, and modern automotive care. The brand identity is built around a strong “AN” lettermark icon, symbolizing accuracy, mechanical expertise, and forward motion. The visual system balances industrial strength with modern clarity, making it suitable for garages, service centers, diagnostics, and automotive maintenance businesses.

​​​​​​​

Brand Challenge

Vehicle service brands must instantly communicate trust, technical skill, and reliability. AutoNex needed a modern yet mechanical identity that feels professional and dependable.
