Brand Overview

Baicev -  EV & Green Mobility Brand Identity

Baicev is a next-generation EV and green mobility brand focused on sustainable transportation, smart electric systems, and future-ready mobility solutions. The brand aims to create a clean, efficient, and intelligent transportation ecosystem that aligns with modern environmental and technological advancements.

The goal of Baicev is to represent innovation, reliability, and sustainability through a minimal yet futuristic brand identity that can work across digital platforms, vehicles, charging infrastructure, and smart mobility solutions.

Brand Challenge

The EV and green mobility industry is growing rapidly, making it increasingly competitive for new brands to establish a distinct visual identity. Baicev required a modern, scalable, and memorable brand identity.

The challenge was to design a logo and identity that balances technology, sustainability, and automotive professionalism while maintaining simplicity and scalability.

Logo Concept & Idea

The Baicev logo is built around a BC lettermark icon, designed with clean geometry and smooth motion-inspired forms.  The sharp yet smooth letter construction creates a balance between technology and sustainability, making the identity suitable for EV startups, charging solutions, and smart mobility platforms.

The icon is designed to remain recognizable even at small sizes, ensuring flexibility across mobile apps, vehicle branding, and digital interfaces.

Color Strategy

The Baicev color system is designed to reflect electric mobility, innovation, and sustainability.

Primary Color - Electric Sky Blue (#1EB4EB)

This color represents:

⦾ Electric energy,

⦾ Innovation and technology,

⦾ Smart mobility solutions

It creates a clean, modern, and futuristic brand presence.

Secondary Colors - Deep Tech Blue (#063454)

Represents:

⦾ Trust and reliability

⦾ Technology infrastructure

⦾ Professional automotive services

Dark Midnight (#021122)

Represents:

⦾ Premium automotive feel

⦾ Futuristic environment

⦾ Strong brand foundation
