Brand Overview

FluxEV - Electric Vehicle & Green Mobility Brand Identity

FluxEV is a future-focused electric mobility brand built around clean energy, innovation, and sustainable transportation.

The identity system emphasizes energy flow, motion, and technological precision through a solid-first logo, electric-inspired color palette, and scalable visual language. Designed to perform across EV infrastructure, digital platforms, and future mobility ecosystems, FluxEV reflects a cleaner, smarter approach to transportation.

Challenge & Solution

Creating a modern EV identity that communicates innovation, trust, and environmental responsibility while remaining scalable across physical and digital touchpoints.

A solid-first logo system combined with an electric color palette and modular visual language ensures clarity, consistency, and future-proof usability.
