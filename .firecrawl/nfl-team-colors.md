The NFL has 2 conferences – National Football Conference (NFC) and American Football Conference (AFC). 16 NFL teams play in the National Football Conference and 16 NFL teams play in the American Football Conference.

![NFL colors](https://teamcolorcodes.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/12/NFL-logo-218x300.png)

## What Are the Meanings of the Colors Used by NFL Teams?

The meanings of NFL Team Colors are listed below.

01. [Baltimore Ravens](https://teamcolorcodes.com/baltimore-ravens-color-codes/) – The Baltimore Ravens colors (purple, black, gold, red) symbolize the raven bird and pay tribute to the state flag of Maryland.
02. [Cincinnati Bengals](https://teamcolorcodes.com/cincinnati-bengals-color-codes/) – The Cincinnati Bengals colors (orange, black) pay tribute to the colors of a tiger.
03. [Cleveland Browns](https://teamcolorcodes.com/cleveland-browns-color-codes/) – The Cleveland Browns colors (brown, orange) pay tribute to the first coach Paul Brown and his last name.
04. [Pittsburgh Steelers](https://teamcolorcodes.com/pittsburgh-steelers-color-codes/) – The Pittsburgh Steelers colors (Steelers gold, black, blue, red, silver) are inspired by the Pittsburgh city flag (gold and black) as well as coal, iron, and steel (materials used to make steel).
05. [Buffalo Bills](https://teamcolorcodes.com/buffalo-bills-color-codes/) – The exact meaning of the Buffalo Bills colors (blue, red) is unknown. The first blue color of the team was inspired by the Detroit Lions colors.
06. [Miami Dolphins](https://teamcolorcodes.com/miami-dolphins-color-codes/) – The Miami Dolphins’ colors (aqua, orange, blue) symbolize the colors of the water and the sunny weather of Florida.
07. [New England Patriots](https://teamcolorcodes.com/new-england-patriots-color-codes/) – The New England Patriots’ colors (nautical blue, red, New Century silver) pay tribute to the United States flag.
08. [New York Jets](https://teamcolorcodes.com/new-york-jets-color-codes/) – The New York Jets colors (Gotham green, Stealth black, Spotlight white) pay tribute to St. Patrick’s Day which was the owner’s Sonny Werblin’s birthday.
09. [Houston Texans](https://teamcolorcodes.com/houston-texans-color-codes/) – The Houston Texans’ colors (Deep Steel blue, Battle red) pay tribute to the state flag of Texas.
10. [Indianapolis Colts](https://teamcolorcodes.com/indianapolis-colts-color-codes/) – The Indianapolis Colts colors (Speed blue, gray) are inspired by the Baltimore Colts colors and the defunct Dallas Texans before them.
11. [Jacksonville Jaguars](https://teamcolorcodes.com/jacksonville-jaguars-color-codes/) – The Jacksonville Jaguars’ colors (black, gold, dark gold, teal) pay tribute to the jaguar (animal) and the team’s unique heritage (Jaguars teal color).
12. [Tennessee Titans](https://teamcolorcodes.com/tennessee-titans-color-codes/) – The Tennessee Titans colors (Titans navy, Titans blue, Titans red, Titans silver) pay tribute to the flags of Tennessee and the United States.
13. [Denver Broncos](https://teamcolorcodes.com/denver-broncos-color-codes/) – The Denver Broncos colors (Broncos orange, Broncos navy) are inspired by the Cleveland Browns colors.
14. [Kansas City Chiefs](https://teamcolorcodes.com/kansas-city-chiefs-color-codes/) – The Kansas City Chiefs’ colors (red, gold) pay tribute to the team and city heritage.
15. [Las Vegas Raiders](https://teamcolorcodes.com/oakland-raiders-color-codes/) – The Las Vegas Raiders colors (Raiders black, Raiders silver) symbolize the traditional pirate colors and the team’s history.
16. [Los Angeles Chargers](https://teamcolorcodes.com/los-angeles-chargers-color-codes/) – The Los Angeles Chargers’ colors (Powder blue, Sunshine gold, white) pay tribute to lightning and the team’s famous history.
17. [Chicago Bears](https://teamcolorcodes.com/chicago-bears-color-codes/) – The Chicago Bears’ colors (Dark navy, orange) are inspired by the colors of the University of Illinois.
18. [Detroit Lions](https://teamcolorcodes.com/detroit-lions-color-codes/) – The Detroit Lions’ colors (Honolulu blue, silver, black, white) pay tribute to Hawaii and the Pacific Ocean.
19. [Green Bay Packers](https://teamcolorcodes.com/green-bay-packers-color-codes/) – The Green Bay Packers colors (Dark green, gold) initially paid tribute to the Notre Dame Irish colors and then to the city of Green Bay.
20. [Minnesota Vikings](https://teamcolorcodes.com/minnesota-vikings-color-codes/) – The Minnesota Vikings’ colors (purple, gold) pay tribute to the University of Washington and Minnesota’s Scandinavian tradition.
21. [Dallas Cowboys](https://teamcolorcodes.com/dallas-cowboys-color-codes/) – The Dallas Cowboys colors (royal blue, blue, silver, silver-green, white) symbolize the team’s history and the creativity of the first Dallas Cowboys GM Tex Schramm.
22. [New York Giants](https://teamcolorcodes.com/new-york-giants-color-codes/) – The exact meaning of the New York Giants colors (dark blue, red, gray) is not completely known. The colors have remained almost unchanged since the foundation.
23. [Philadelphia Eagles](https://teamcolorcodes.com/philadelphia-eagles-color-codes/) – The exact meaning of the Philadelphia Eagles colors (Midnight green, silver, black, charcoal) is not completely known.
24. [Washington Commanders](https://teamcolorcodes.com/washington-redskins-color-codes/) – The Washington Commanders colors (burgundy, gold) were chosen by the team’s first owner George Preston Marshall to symbolize class and creativity.
25. [Atlanta Falcons](https://teamcolorcodes.com/atlanta-falcons-color-codes/) – The Atlanta Falcons colors (red, black, silver) symbolize two famous Georgia schools – the Georgia Tech Yellow Jackets and the Georgia Bulldogs.
26. [Carolina Panthers](https://teamcolorcodes.com/carolina-panthers-color-codes/) – The Carolina Panthers colors (Carolina blue, black, silver) are inspired by Duke and the UNC, with the Carolina Panthers’ Process Blue shade being between the two famous university colors.
27. [New Orleans Saints](https://teamcolorcodes.com/new-orleans-saints-color-codes/) – The New Orleans Saints colors (Old gold, black) symbolize Mardi Gras and oil.
28. [Tampa Bay Buccaneers](https://teamcolorcodes.com/tampa-bay-buccaneers-team-colors/) – The Tampa Bay Buccaneers’ colors (red, Bay orange, black, gray, pewter) pay tribute to the traditional buccaneer (pirate) colors.
29. [Arizona Cardinals](https://teamcolorcodes.com/arizona-cardinals-color-codes/) – The Arizona Cardinals’ colors (red, black, yellow) pay tribute to the first used jerseys that the team wore – they were faded red, a color which became known as Cardinal red.
30. [Los Angeles Rams](https://teamcolorcodes.com/los-angeles-rams-team-colors/) – The exact meaning of the Los Angeles Rams colors (blue, gold, dark gold, yellow, white) is not known.
31. [San Francisco 49ers](https://teamcolorcodes.com/san-francisco-49ers-team-colors/) – The San Francisco 49ers colors (49ers red, gold) symbolize the California Gold Rush and the city of San Francisco.
32. [Seattle Seahawks](https://teamcolorcodes.com/seattle-seahawks-color-codes/) – The Seattle Seahawks colors (College navy, Action green, Wolf gray) pay tribute to the traditional colors of the Pacific Northwest.
