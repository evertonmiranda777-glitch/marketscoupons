## Step ONE - Choose your drawdown type

[Intraday\\
\\
An Intraday Trailing Maximum Drawdown (Intraday TMD) calculates the maximum drawdown Limit from unrealized profits in real-time during a trading session. This drawdown will freeze at the account starting balance.](https://www.tradeday.com/our-pricing#w-tabs-0-data-w-pane-0) [End of Day\\
\\
An End of Day Trailing Maximum Drawdown Limit (EOD TMD) calculates the maximum drawdown limit from the realized profits on the end-of-day balance (at the 16.00 CT futures market close). This drawdown will freeze at the account starting balance.](https://www.tradeday.com/our-pricing#w-tabs-0-data-w-pane-1) [Static\\
\\
A static drawdown limit (SDL) is a fixed maximum drawdown limit that does not move or track your account balance growth.](https://www.tradeday.com/our-pricing#w-tabs-0-data-w-pane-2)
